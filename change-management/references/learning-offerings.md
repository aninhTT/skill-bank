# Learning Offerings at Thumbtack
Source: AI Tooling and Support Resources Update (March 2026)

---

## Programs by Learning Stage

### Explorer → TackStart
**Program:** TackStart — 30 Days of Exploring AI
**What it is:** Foundational AI fluency. Takes people from basics to building custom GPTs and NotebookLM workflows. Designed for people who are just getting started or haven't found a consistent habit yet.
**Format:** Cohort-based; lessons are designed to be 5–15 minutes per day
**Cohort schedule:** Starts every two weeks. Next cohorts: 3/16, 3/30, 4/13
**How to sign up:** Sign up link in AI Hub
**Best for:** Anyone at Explorer stage; teams where most people haven't developed a consistent practice

---

### Operator → TackShift
**Program:** TackShift — 30 Days of Operating Better with AI
**What it is:** Rethinking workflows and moving toward higher-leverage use of automation and agents. For people who are already using AI but want to go deeper and build repeatable systems.
**Format:** Cohort-based; same cadence as TackStart
**Cohort schedule:** Starts every two weeks (same cohort dates as TackStart)
**How to sign up:** Sign up link in AI Hub
**Best for:** People at Operator stage who want to move toward Builder; teams that have some adoption but want to level up

---

### Builder → Targeted Learning Sessions
**Program:** Targeted Learning Sessions
**What it is:** Deeper skills in specific areas — agent-building, Claude Code for non-developers, advanced automation techniques. Content is more hands-on and technical than TackStart/TackShift.
**Format:** Scheduled sessions (calendar of upcoming events in AI Hub)
**How to sign up:** AI Hub events calendar / Updated skill calendar
**Best for:** People at Operator moving to Builder; anyone who wants to build their first agent or skill; non-developers who want to explore Claude Code

---

### All Stages → Core Resources

**Claude Enterprise Learning Resources**
Claude Cowork/Code personal tutor that runs directly in Claude. Helps people learn how to use the Claude tools effectively through conversation.
Access instructions in AI Hub.

**AI in the Workplace Training**
General training to help employees better understand how to safely use AI at Thumbtack. Covers responsible use, AI/ML usage policy, and governance basics.
Available in AI Hub. (Note: this training is required for all employees.)

## Key Resources to Always Point People To

**AI Hub**
The central place for everything AI at Thumbtack. Always start here. Includes: quick start guide, tool access directory, learning program sign-ups, and event calendar.

**#ai-lab (Slack)**
Use this channel to share wins (big or small), before/after workflow improvements, lessons from things that didn't work, questions or requests for help, and tips worth sharing. This is where learning compounds across the company.

**#ai-resources (Slack)**
Company updates on AI, major industry news, relevant releases worth keeping an eye on.

**AI Manager Playbook**
go/manager-ai — practical guidance for managers on how to bring AI into team rhythms, coach team members by learning mode, and run team experimentation sessions.

**Workflow Redesign Pilot**
A select number of teams have been identified to redesign real workflows end-to-end and scale impact across teams. Contact Amie to learn more or explore if your team could participate.
