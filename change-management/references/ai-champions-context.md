# AI Champions Context
Source: AI Tooling Update (March 2026) + Champions Day expectations

## What AI Champions Are

AI Champions are a newly redesigned network of individuals at Thumbtack whose role is to help support AI adoption within their function. They are not full-time AI roles — they are people who care about advancing AI in their area and have committed to the program.

**Core responsibilities:**
- Help route AI work within their department
- Surface patterns and blockers early — both from their team up to the AI team, and from the AI team back to their function
- Build and maintain a functional enablement plan
- Be a visible AI presence within their function

## What Comes After AI Champions Day

After AI Champions Day, the clear expectation is:

> "Your next step is to turn around and think about how to best enable your function — given the momentum and energy happening right now. This is an expectation for all Champions."

Amie will set up time with each **functional group** (e.g., Product, People, Finance, Legal, Marketing) to work through enablement plans together. These sessions are collaborative — not evaluative.

**Plans can look different by function:**
- Some champions may take the existing structure and content Amie has already built and adapt it for their team — that's great.
- Others may want to create something more bespoke for their function — also great.
- Some functions (e.g., Engineering) are already actively tackling this challenge with their own plan — also fine.

Either way, champions will have support to figure out what makes sense for their context.

## How to Frame the Champion Role to Your Function

Champions are a bridge — they help their function understand what's available, what's working elsewhere, and what to try. They're not experts yet. They're committed learners who are willing to go first and share what they learn.
